# Dataset: Retail Sales

## Source Summary
Daily retail sales transactions across four regions and three customer segments.

## Metric Definitions
revenue = gross sales revenue after discount
units_sold = number of items sold
discount_pct = percent discount applied to the sale

## Business Rules
- revenue should be interpreted at the daily transaction grain
- posted_at is the trusted transaction date

## Trusted Date Fields
- posted_at

## Preferred Identifiers
- region
- segment
- channel
